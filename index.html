Mercury

Mercury is the smallest planet in our solar system and the one closest to the Sun. Despite its proximity to the Sun, it is not the hottest planet, as Venus retains more heat due to its thick atmosphere.
Mercury has a thin atmosphere, known as an exosphere, composed mainly of oxygen, sodium, hydrogen, helium, and potassium.
This makes the planet unable to retain heat, causing extreme temperature variations between day and night.
